    Who am I?

    My name is Roberto Cardenas, I am a Web Developer based in Berkeley, California. I have worked on and off as a Web Developer for the last 5 years in tandem with my school work, starting off as full stack and over time developing a stronger interest in design and front end development.

    What am I interested in?

    I am interested in building sites that are useful, easy to navigate and beautiful to look at. I believe being able to present content clearly through design is what truly makes or breaks a website.
